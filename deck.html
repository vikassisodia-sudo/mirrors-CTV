07 · Challenges
Why Content Reporting is Challenging

Understanding the structural limitations behind CTV content reporting

Click to explore
🛡️
Publisher Data Quality Varies
Publishers differ in how they populate the OpenRTB content object — some share complete metadata (Full Data), others share only IDs (Bare Minimum), or put Series + Episode inside the wrong fields (Mixed-Up).
✓ Full Data ⚡ Partial ⚠ Mixed-Up 🚫 Bare Min
🖥️
TTD Content Dimensions — Publisher Dependent
TTD DSP supports Title and Series as reporting dimensions. However, since most publishers do not share complete or correctly structured content parameters, values are incomplete or unavailable for a large share of impressions.
Result: Content dimensions exist in TTD but cannot be reliably populated without publisher cooperation.
🔍
No Win/Loss Reporting for Data Partners
As a data partner, Silverpush does not receive impression win/loss reports from TTD for every bid request. This means it is not possible to back-map and confirm the actual content of impressions that were won — making content-level attribution fundamentally limited.
Impact: Cannot reconcile enrichment signals with actual delivered impressions.
📊
Only 2–8% Impressions Have Content Details
Based on past campaign analysis, only 2% – 8% of all impressions received content details. The remaining 92–98% lacked the necessary parameters to identify the content being played at the time of the ad.
